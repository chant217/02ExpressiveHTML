The Story of Bonnie and Clyde

  1. You´ve heard the story of Jesse James
    Of how he lived and died
    If you´re still in need
    Of something to read
    Here´s the story of Bonnie and Clyde...

  2. Now Bonnie and Clyde are the Barrow gang
    I´m sure you all have read
    How they rob and steal
    And those who squeal
    Are usually found dyin´ or dead.

  3. They call them cold-hearted killers
    They say they are heartless and mean
    But I say this with pride
    That I once knew Clyde
    When he was honest and upright and clean.

  4. But ´laws´ fooled around
    Kept takin´ him down
    And lockin´ him up in a cell
    Till he said to me: ´I´ll never be free
    So I´ll meet a few of them in Hell.´

  5. If a policeman is killed in Dallas
    And they have no clue to guide
    If they can´t find a fiend
    They just wipe their slate clean
    And hang it on Bonnie and Clyde.

  6. If they try to act like citizens
    And rent them a nice little flat
    About the third night
    They´re invited to fight
    By a sub-guns´ rat-a-tat-tat.

  7. Some day, they´ll go down together
    They´ll bury them side by side
    To a few, it´ll be grief-
    To the law, a relief-
    But it´s death for Bonnie and Clyde.
